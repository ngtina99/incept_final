Rowan and Cloudberry

Rowan

Rowan berries grow on the rowan tree, known for its beauty and folklore significance. They are bright red and appear in clusters, often used in jams and jellies.

Cloudberry

Cloudberries are golden-yellow berries that grow in northern climates. They have a tart taste and are often used in Scandinavian desserts.
